# Breaking News Skill

Breaking news requires rapid assessment and decisive action.

## Breaking News Triage

### Step 1: Assess Impact (30 seconds)
```
1. Source: Reuters/Bloomberg = High credibility
2. Scope: Global, regional, or asset-specific
3. Type: Economic, political, corporate, natural
4. Expected duration: Temporary or structural
```

### Step 2: Impact Classification
| Type | Duration | Examples | Trading |
|------|----------|----------|--------|
| Flash | Hours | Surprise data | Fade or trade spike |
| Short-term | Days | Rate cut | Follow momentum |
| Structural | Weeks+ | Trade war | Trend trade |
| Black Swan | Unknown | Financial crisis | Risk-off everything |

### Step 3: Market Response Check
- **Risk-on assets falling** → All risk assets at risk
- **Safe haven rising** → Capital flight
- **Correlated pairs** → Check majors for direction
- **Volatility spiking** → Reduce positions

## Decision Matrix

| Scenario | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Major trend confirmation | Add to position |
| Trend reversal signal | Close positions, consider reversal |
| Fake-out (reverses fast) | Wait, avoid chasing |
| Extended volatility | Reduce, widen stops |
| Safe haven bid | Exit risk, buy USD/JPY/CHF/GOLD |

## Breaking News Rules

1. **Don't chase** - Wait for the initial spike to settle
2. **Don't assume** - Wait for market to digest
3. **Check correlations** - See how related assets respond
4. **Time horizon** - Is this temporary or structural?
5. **Position sizing** - Reduce in high volatility
6. **Set alerts** - Monitor for follow-through

## Confidence Scoring

| Factor | Weight |
|--------|--------|
| Source reliability (Reuters/Bloomberg) | +30% |
| Multiple sources confirming | +25% |
| Market already responding | +20% |
| Historical precedent | +15% |
| Severity of event | +10% |

## Breaking News Checklist
- [ ] What happened? (Who, what, when, where)
- [ ] Is it confirmed? (Source credibility)
- [ ] Is it new or already priced in?
- [ ] What asset class is affected?
- [ ] What is the likely duration?
- [ ] What are correlated assets doing?
- [ ] What is my position exposure?
- [ ] Do I need to act now or wait?

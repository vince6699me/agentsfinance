# SMC Silver Bullet Skill

The Silver Bullet is a high-probability SMC pattern combining 4 key elements in sequence.

## Pattern Sequence

```
Step 1: Liquidity Grab
         ↓
Step 2: Order Block Re-test
         ↓
Step 3: Fair Value Gap Formation
         ↓
Step 4: Momentum Continuation
```

## Step-by-Step Breakdown

### Step 1: Liquidity Grab
- Price sweeps above/below a key level (swing high/low, range edge)
- Creates stop liquidity for institutions
- Look for: wick extending through level, quick reversal

### Step 2: Order Block Re-test
- Price returns to the OB created before the liquidity grab
- Forms the "Silver Bullet" entry zone
- Wait for price to reach the OB area

### Step 3: Fair Value Gap
- Small imbalance forms as price leaves the OB
- Acts as confirmation and entry trigger
- The FVG confirms institutional order flow

### Step 4: Momentum Continuation
- Strong candle breaks structure in direction of trade
- Large candle following the FVG confirms entry
- Ride the momentum to next structure level

## Trading Rules

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Entry | After FVG confirmation candle |
| Stop Loss | Below/above OB extreme (max 1.5 ATR) |
| TP 1 | Next structure level (1:1) |
| TP 2 | Next OB or liquidity pool (2:1) |
| Risk:Reward | Minimum 3:1 |

## Confidence Scoring

| Factor | Weight |
|--------|--------|
| All 4 steps present and clear | +40% |
| Located in premium/discount zone | +20% |
| Kill zone timing | +15% |
| Higher timeframe alignment | +15% |
| Clean price action (no chop) | +10% |

## Example

```
GBPUSD H1 Silver Bullet:
Step 1: Price grabs liquidity above 1.2800 (swing high)
Step 2: Returns to bullish OB at 1.2780-1.2785
Step 3: Small FVG forms at 1.2788-1.2792
Step 4: Strong bullish candle breaks 1.2800

Entry: 1.2795 (after confirmation candle)
Stop: 1.2765 (30 pips)
TP1: 1.2825 (1:1)
TP2: 1.2860 (2:1)
Confidence: 88%
```

# Fundamental Regime Skill

Macroeconomic regimes determine which trading strategies perform best.

## Regime Types

| Regime | Characteristics | Best Trades |
|--------|----------------|-------------|
| **Growth** | GDP rising, employment strong | Risk-on, equities, commodities |
| **Inflationary** | CPI rising, rates up | Real assets, commodities, short bonds |
| **Stagflation** | Low growth + high inflation | Commodities, gold, defensive stocks |
| **Deflation** | Prices falling, growth negative | Cash, bonds, defensive long |
| **Recession** | GDP negative, unemployment rising | Cash, quality bonds, defensive |

## Key Indicators

| Indicator | Source | Impact |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| GDP Growth | FRED | Long-term trend |
| CPI Inflation | FRED/BLS | Fed policy driver |
| Fed Funds Rate | FRED | Cost of capital |
| PMI | ISM/FRED | Leading growth indicator |
| Employment | BLS | Consumption driver |
| Yield Curve | FRED | Recession predictor |

## Regime Detection Rules

### Expansion/Growth Regime
- GDP YoY > 2% → Growth regime
- CPI 2-3% → Normal
- Fed Funds 2-5% → Accommodative/neutral
- PMI > 50 → Expanding

### Inflationary Regime
- CPI > 3% → Inflation concerns
- Fed Funds rising → Tightening
- PPI rising → Input price pressure
- Commodities trending up

### Recession Regime
- GDP QoQ < 0% for 2+ quarters
- Unemployment rising
- Yield curve inverted
- PMI < 45

## Trading Rules by Regime

### Risk-On (Growth)
- Buy equities, sell USD, buy EM
- AUD, NZD, CAD benefit
- High beta stocks outperform

### Risk-Off (Recession)
- Buy USD, CHF, JPY
- Buy government bonds
- Gold outperforms
- Low beta/defensive stocks

### Inflation Hedge
- Buy commodities (especially gold)
- Buy TIPS (inflation-protected bonds)
- Short duration bonds
- Real assets outperform

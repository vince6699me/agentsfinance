# Fundamental Rates Skill

Interest rates are the primary driver of currency valuations in forex.

## Key Rate Concepts

### Interest Rate Differential
- **Positive differential**: Higher rates attract capital → currency appreciation
- **Negative differential**: Lower rates lose carry → currency depreciation
- Monitor 2Y and 10Y yields across currency pairs

### Carry Trade
- Borrow in low-rate currency
- Invest in high-rate currency
- Collect interest differential
- Risk: Rate convergence, risk-off flows

## Central Bank Analysis

### Fed (Federal Reserve)
- Dual mandate: Price stability + Maximum employment
- Watch: FOMC statements, Fed minutes, Fed speakers
- Key rates: Fed Funds Rate (current: check FRED)
- Curve: 2Y (rate expectations), 10Y (growth/inflation)

### ECB (European Central Bank)
- Primary mandate: Price stability (<2%)
- Watch: ECB Rate Decision, Lagarde speeches
- Key rates: Main Refinancing Rate, Deposit Facility Rate
- Balance sheet: APP, PEPP programs

### BoE (Bank of England)
- Inflation targeting with 2% goal
- Watch: MPC votes, Governor speeches
- Key rates: Bank Rate
- Focus: Inflation response

## Rate Decision Trading

### Dovish vs Hawkish
| Tone | Meaning | Market Reaction |
|------|---------|----------------|
| Dovish | Rates to stay low | Currency falls, bonds rally |
| Hawkish | Rates to rise | Currency rises, bonds fall |
| Neutral | No change expected | Minimal reaction |

### Trading the Statement
1. Compare statement to expectations
2. Watch for changes in forward guidance
3. Analyze voting split (dissenters)
4. Rate differential shift after decision

## Yield Curve Analysis

| Curve Shape | Signal |
|------------|--------|
| Normal (2Y < 10Y) | Healthy growth expectations |
| Flat (2Y ≈ 10Y) | Growth concerns emerging |
| Inverted (2Y > 10Y) | Recession warning (6-18 month lead) |
| Steepening | Growth expectations rising |

## Confidence Scoring

| Factor | Weight |

| Forward guidance shift | +25% |
| Voting unanimous vs divided | +15% |
| Dot plot changes | +15% |

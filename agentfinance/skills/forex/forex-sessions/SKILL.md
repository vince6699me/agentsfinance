# Forex Sessions Skill

Session-based analysis for optimal forex trading timing.

## Major Trading Sessions

### Sydney Session
- **UTC**: 22:00-07:00
- **Volatility**: Low
- **Pairs**: AUD, NZD
- **Characteristics**: Quiet, range-bound, breakout potential

### Tokyo Session
- **UTC**: 00:00-09:00
- **Volatility**: Medium
- **Pairs**: JPY, AUD
- **Characteristics**: Range-bound, JPY volatility

### London Session
- **UTC**: 07:00-16:00
- **Volatility**: High
- **Pairs**: EUR, GBP, CHF
- **Characteristics**: Trending, high volume

### New York Session
- **UTC**: 12:00-21:00
- **Volatility**: Very High
- **Pairs**: All majors
- **Characteristics**: Volatile, trending

## Session Overlaps

### London-NY Overlap (12:00-16:00 UTC)
- Highest volatility of the day
- Best for momentum trades
- Spreads tightest
- Avoid first 30 min (chop)

### Best Trading Windows
| Window | UTC | Quality |
|--------|-----|---------|
| London Open | 07:00-09:00 | High |
| London-NY Overlap | 12:00-14:00 | Very High |
| NY Open | 12:00-14:00 | High |
| London Close | 14:00-16:00 | Medium |

## Worst Trading Windows
| Window | UTC | Quality |
|--------|-----|---------|
| NY Close | 19:00-21:00 | Low |
| Asia Open | 22:00-00:00 | Low |
| Light NY/London | 15:00-17:00 | Medium |

## Session-Based Rules
- **Momentum trades**: London/NY open, overlap
- **Range trades**: Asia, London close
- **Breakout trades**: Session opens
- **Scalping**: Overlap periods

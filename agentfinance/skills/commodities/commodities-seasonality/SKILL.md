## Seasonal Trading Rules

### Gold
1. **Buy**: September weakness → October recovery
2. **Buy**: January post-holiday → February setup
3. **Sell**: Late August strength → September weakness
4. **Best months**: August, November, April

### Oil
1. **Buy**: October/November → Pre-winter demand
2. **Sell**: January/February → Post-holiday slack
3. **Buy**: March/April → Driving season
4. **Sell**: June/September → Peak/transition

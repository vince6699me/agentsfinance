# Commodities SMC Skill

Applying SMC to commodity markets with specialized analysis.

## Key Commodities

### Gold (XAUUSD)
- Safe haven asset
- Inverse correlation with USD
- Responds to real yields, inflation
- High volatility during risk events

### Crude Oil (WTI/Brent)
- Driven by supply/demand
- OPEC announcements impact
- Seasonal patterns
- Correlated with risk appetite

### Silver (XAGUSD)
- Industrial + monetary metal
- More volatile than gold
- Follows gold direction
- Higher correlation with equities

## Commodity-Specific SMC

### Gold Trading
- OB often forms at round numbers
- Watch gold/silver ratio for clues
- Real yield direction key driver
- SMC works well on H4/Daily

### Oil Trading
- Supply/demand creates unique OBs
- Inventory reports drive spikes
- Range-bound periods common
- Seasonal tendencies

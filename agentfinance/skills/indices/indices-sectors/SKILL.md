## Sector Rotation Model

### Economic Cycle
| Phase | Leading | Lagging |
|-------|---------|---------|
| Early Recovery | Tech, Consumer Disc. | Utilities, Staples |
| Mid Cycle | Financials, Industrials | Tech |
| Late Cycle | Energy, Materials | Consumer Disc. |
| Contraction | Utilities, Staples | Tech, Consumer Disc. |

## Sector Momentum

### Relative Strength Ranking
1. Rank sectors by 20-day performance
2. Long top performers vs bottom
3. Rotate quarterly at minimum
4. Rebalance monthly

## Trading Rules
1. Overweight leading sectors in bull markets
2. Rotate to defensive in late cycle
3. Use sector ETFs for precise exposure
4. Sector allocation should match conviction
5. Rebalance quarterly minimum
